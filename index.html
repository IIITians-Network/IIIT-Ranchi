    IIIT Ranchi


    FOUNDATION

    Indian Institute of Information Technology Ranchi is an autonomous institute was established in the year 2016, setup by the MHRD, Government of India, and Government of Jharkhand along with the industry partners Central Coalfield Limitd (CCL) , Tata Consultancy Services (TCS) and Tata Technologies on a Public Private Partnership. It is an "Institute of National Importance", declared by an act of parliament. The IIIT Ranchi is funded by Government of India (50%), Government of Jharkhand (35%) and Industry Partners (15%).

    STUDENT LIFE

    IIIT Ranchi will focus on applied research and education in IT and in selected domain areas. IIIT Ranchi is committed to academic excellence and good governance. Apart from the regular curriculum of the institute, the students are given a plethora of opportunities to showcase and build their talent. The various technical and cultural clubs like CuLAरीति- Culatral Society, House of Geeks- Technical Society, Sports Council, EBSB Chapter- IIIT Ranchi, The Optical Society of America-OSA Chapter, IIIT Ranchi are blooming with events for everyone to participate and cherish that help the students to grow their domain in the fields of Tech, Arts, Culture, Leadership and Sportsmanship. A good administration and efficient students' council is a part of an able institution. Student' Council represents the opinions, ideas and proposals of the students. It consists of 15 members. In 2019, Prabhat Suman was selected as the President of Students' Council, IIIT Ranchi.

    Department of Computer Science and Engineering

    The Department of Computer Science & Engineering of IIIT Ranchi offers Bachelor of Technology (B.Tech.) course in Computer Science & Engineering. The programme is designed to create innovators and researchers. Students are given a strong foundation in CSE that includes Coding, Development, Data Structure & Algorithm.

    Department of Electronics & Communication Engineering

    The department of Electronics & Communication Engineering of IIIT Ranchi offers Bachelor in Technology (B.Tech.) course in Electronics & Communication Engineering. The programme is designed to create innovators and researchers. ECE courses like electronics, signals & systems, Network Theory, communications, VLSI, and embedded systems are introduced in the early semesters.

    Department of M.Tech. in ML/Data Science & Embedded systems/IOT

    Post Graduate in Machine Learning and Data Science- IIIT Ranchi offers Master of Technology (M.Tech.) course in Machine Learning and Data Science from academic session 2019-2020. Post Graduate in Embedded systems and IOT- IIIT Ranchi offers Master of Technology (M.Tech.) course in Embedded systems and IOT from academic session 2019-2020.

    Department of Ph.D in CSE and ECE branches

    The institute also offers Ph.D course in CSE and ECE branches. The Department of Computer Science & Engineering of IIIT Ranchi offers Ph.D. in Computer Science & Engineering. The department of Electronics & Communication Engineering of IIIT Ranchi offers Ph.D. in Electronics & Communication Engineering.

    INSTITUTE INDUSTRY INTERACTION- A Leap Towards Excellence

    Indian Institute of Information Technology, Ranchi, organised it's first Institute-industrial interaction. This program was organized by the Training and Placement Cell IIIT Ranchi. The guest panel comprised of the Director of the institute Dr Vishnu Priye, and technical experts from top companies:
    1) Anuranjan Yadav (Walmart)
    2) B. Muralitharan (Hexaware)
    3) Rishikesh Singh (Deloitte)
    4) Neel Biswas (Samsung R&D)
    5) Rachit Jain(Towered Research Capital)
    The interactive session served not only as an updation regarding present scenario in the industry, but also as a prelude to the forthcoming advancements in the field of information technology. The interactive-session witnessed some blazing questions from the students and a lot of enthusiasm from the Students.

    Hindi Diwas

    Since 1953, 14th September is celebrated as "Hindi Diwas" in all over India. IIIT Ranchi commemorates this holy language that has kept our country united and will do so for ages to come on the Hindi Diwas. Speech competition, debate competition and poetry recitation were organized on the auspicious occasion of Hindi Day in which many students participated. The winners of the speech competition were declared "Abhishek Kumar" (second year) and the winner of the debate competition "Sudhir Kumar" (third year).
